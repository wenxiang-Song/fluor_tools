About Fluor-tools

Fluor-tools is a unified platform integrating two specialized modules: NIRFluor-opt and Fluor-RLAT. The NIRFluor-opt module focuses on optimizing the structure of near-infrared fluorescent dye molecules, while the Fluor-RLAT module is designed for accurate property prediction in various solvents. This platform leverages the power of AI and cheminformatics to accelerate research in fluorescence-based materials.
